Fergus Alistair Allan - Letters

 

AUS 401265  Pilot Officer  ALLAN F.A.

Royal Australian Air Force

C/- R.A.F.  H.Q.

MIDDLE EAST

 

 

25th September 1941

 

 

Social Telegram

 

 

 

 VIA IMPERIAL 1560 ABROAD                               25th

 

EFM                MRS D L ALLAN

                        17 DREWETT ST

                        SURREY HILLS   VIC

 

AL WELL AND SAFE YOU ARE MORE THAN EVER IN MY THOUGHTS AT THIS TIME.  FONDEST LOVE.

 

                                    FERGUS ALLAN

 

                                                9.44 am
