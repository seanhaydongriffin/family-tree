  • Name: John WALKER
  • Sex: M
  • Birth: 1832 in Almondsbury, Gloucestershire, England
  • Death: WFT Est. 1876-1924
  • Note:
    When John Walker died in July 1874, he left a family of 4 children by his first wife Rhoda and 4 children by his second wife Elizabeth (nee Vallis). Elizabeth's 4 children were still quite young for a woman on her own to be sole provider for them.

    In 1874 Elizabeth married Charles McLaughan. Charles McLaughlan had also been named as a witness at the death of John Walker, so Charles was obviously a local man.

    He arrived in NSW about 1854, around the same time as our John and Rhoda, and married Elizabeth at age 24. Also there were 5 children of this marraige. James b: 1875, Nathaniel b: 1882, Irene b: 1892 plus one male and one female deceased.
